  • Civic Tech Waterloo Region

    We're a community group that brings together people from different sectors and industries to actively solve issues facing our local community using technology-based solutions.

  • What is Civic Tech?

    Technology meets public good

    Civic Technology is technology that enables engagement or participation of the public for stronger development, enhancing citizen communications, improving government infrastructure, and generally improving the public good.

  • Who is it for?

    Come join us

    We welcome everyone who is interested in better understanding and finding solutions to civic challenges through technology, design or other means. People of all backgrounds and skill levels are welcome to attend.

Events

We meet every two weeks, alternating between speaker sessions and hacknights. Our speakers are community leaders such as someone from a local non-profit, government, industry, or any enaged citizen - what matters is that they have something inspiring or educational to share with our group. During both our speaker sessions and our hacknights, we also provide space and time for people to come and work together on civic tech projects. Join us at our next hacknight!
